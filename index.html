what i may do

When I first decided to study in UK, my original choice was mathematics or physics,
precisely it is applied mathematics or quantum physics because I am not only
good at these two subjects but also very interested in both. I thought that we
should learn because of interest, which means you want to get knowledge yourself
but not for other reasons. But now I have changed my mind, I am almost an adult
and I have to face a practical fact—money. If I specialize in mathematic or physics,
I may just make a teacher in the future but it is a fact that in China the average
salary of the teacher is lower than the per capita income, at the lower and middle
levels of society. So，after a discussion with my mother and teacher, I think EE
(Electronic Engineering) is a good option. Since EE is related to science
departments and I am confident that I can master it as soon as possible. In
additional to future career prospect, electron industry is one of the most popular
vocations, some problems like driverless technology is still under solving. Dislike
mathematics or physics, most of the puzzle problems have been perfectly
explained by the intelligent scientists. Also, with popularity of network, how to
reinforce the quality of the signal will also be a challenge. 5G technology is now
designed by HUAWEI, some facilities have been constructed. But what about 6G？
I think it is what we might do in the future.

airbnb

Last week, I had a program which is to analyze the price of the house in London in Airbnb. That was the first time that I use python to process data though I have just learned using python for one month.
To be honest, I was not willing to join the team at first since I was not so good at programming by using python, I even couldn’t remember the “grammar ” clearly.
But i still do so. When I understand what I am going to do, I was really curious about it. The main job is to draw different kinds of maps to describe the house price in London. At first the teacher help us download the necessary “environment” and some plugins. After hundreds lines of code, I finally finished.
Although it is really a massive and complicated work, when you finish it you will gain a sense of achievement especially you are just new to this. When you are typing codes, you will find the intrinsic beauty as each step is full of logic, you will not make the program work without any step and the wrong order. You need to prepare everything for the next aim, even it looks like something insignificant like a punctuation mark or a blank.
In terms of mathematics, it is absolutely an indispensable part in every areas. When you are programming, you need to follow the logic, and sometimes you need to use the matrix to processing the data. For example I used the matrix function to deal with the population density.
I highly agree that “by studying mathematics, you gain a diverse range of transferable skills” Mathematics is just like the base of the universe , you can use it to explain almost everything. When you study more, you may apply a universal language in a multiplicity of situations cause maths itself is a kind of special language.
It is definitely true that university study is more independent than at school, since it is more difficult. So the time-management skill becomes more essential at this time, you need to allocate time properly in every part. And without a clear aim you will get lost as you don’t know what you want, so determination is also important in university. A good work ethic is the base of the university life, you may not get succeeded easily without these three.
